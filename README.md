# AI-Powered-Inventory-Optimization-System
End-to-end inventory optimization project using Python ,SQL , Power BI, and demand forecasting

Business Problem:
Retail businesses frequently face inventory challenges such as stockouts, overstocking, and inefficient replenishment planning due to inaccurate demand forecasting and limited visibility into inventory performance.
This project aims to optimize inventory management by analyzing historical sales data, monitoring inventory health, evaluating supplier performance, and forecasting future demand to support data-driven replenishment decisions.

Dataset Overview:
The project uses synthetic retail supply chain data consisting of:
100,000+ Sales Transactions
500 Products
50 Suppliers
10 Warehouses
Customer purchase records
Inventory and stock level information
Supplier lead time and rating data
Datasets Used

Dataset	Description:
Sales Transactions	Historical customer purchases and revenue
Products	Product category, cost, and product details
Inventory	Current stock, safety stock, and reorder points
Suppliers	Supplier lead time, ratings, and country
Warehouses	Warehouse capacity information

Technologies Used"
Programming & Analysis
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Scikit-Learn
Database
SQL
Visualization
Power BI
Machine Learning
Linear Regression
Demand Forecasting

Key Insights:
Generated total revenue of 108.48 Million from 340,139 units sold.
Electronics was the highest revenue-contributing category with 21.45% revenue share.
October 2025 recorded the highest demand while February 2025 recorded the lowest demand.
Identified products at risk of stockout and overstock using inventory threshold analysis.
Developed a rule-based inventory recommendation engine for replenishment decisions.
Evaluated supplier performance using lead time and supplier ratings.

Forecast Output:
Predicted Demand for January 2026: 7,387 Units
Business Recommendation
Increase inventory allocation before seasonal demand peaks.
Use forecast-driven replenishment planning to reduce stockout risks.
Continuously retrain forecasting models with updated sales data.
